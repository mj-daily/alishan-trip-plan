Alishan Suggested Day Trip

Welcome to Alishan! Here is a simple one-day itinerary:

  1. Early morning: take the forest railway or a bus to Zhushan for sunrise viewing (weather permitting).
  2. Return and explore Alishan National Scenic Area: Sister Ponds and the giant cypress trail.
  3. Lunch: try local mountain cuisine or a bento box.
  4. Afternoon: visit Fenqihu Old Street for railway bento and traditional snacks.
